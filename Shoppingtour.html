Shopping Tour in Cancun.

Cancun vacation, acquires some recollection from cancun

In Cancún we begin our tour on the famous Market 28, a traditional and picturesque place, known for its wide variety of crafts and gifts. The second part of the route is in the hotel zone of Cancun and we will visit the famous Plaza Kukulcán, a very luxurious and renowned shopping mall in Cancun, where you can find shops with the best brands in the world. The tour will continue to La Isla Shopping Village, where you will find delicious restaurants, clothing stores and perfumes, as well as its famous and interesting Aquarium interactive, full of surprises and dolphins!

Shopping Tour in Playa del Carmen

On vacation in Playa del Carmen, acquires some recollection from Playa del Carmen

In Playa del Carmen you can experience the fantastic 5th Avenue, well-known cosmopolitan place where you can meet people from all over the world, while enjoying a leisurely stroll by shops of renowned brands, craft stores, and of course, the most prestigious restaurants in the area.
