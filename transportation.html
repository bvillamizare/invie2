Adapted transportation

Regular transportation and especial transportation with ramps for travelers with reduced mobility. We offer transfers from the airport to all Hotels in Cancun, Playa del Carmen and The Riviera Maya

Special transfer with wheelchair ramp

Private transportation in a van accessible to handicapped persons and those with reduced mobility, with air conditioning, Our vans allow our passengers in wheelchairs to travel, if they prefer in their own chair.

In Handicapped services, we really recognize the importance of every person; no matter if they have limitations or not; we offer many good options and services for travelers with special needs; in order to allow them to enjoy all Caribbean in a comfortable and safe way.
